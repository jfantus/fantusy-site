Calculator

Prostate volume and PSA density.

Enter prostate width, height, and length in centimeters. The calculator reports ellipsoid and bullet volume estimates, with optional PSA density if a PSA value is entered.

Inputs

Ellipsoid --

W x H x L x 0.523

PSA density
Enter PSA
Bullet --

W x H x L x 0.65

PSA density
Enter PSA

Formula Notes

Ellipsoid volume = W x H x L x 0.523 Bullet volume = W x H x L x 0.65 PSA density = PSA / volume

This tool is for education and quick estimation. Clinical decisions should use the measurement method, imaging context, and physician judgment appropriate to the patient.
