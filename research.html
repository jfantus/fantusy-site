Research

Men's health, microsurgery, sexual medicine, and clinical systems.

My research interests sit where patient goals, surgical decision-making, and measurable outcomes intersect. Much of the work centers on testosterone, fertility, sexual medicine, vasal reconstruction, urologic trauma, and how technology changes care delivery.

01

Testosterone And Men's Health

Population-level work on testosterone reference ranges, metabolic health, aging, and the practical implications of diagnosing and treating testosterone deficiency.

02

Male Fertility And Microsurgery

Research and writing on vasectomy reversal, vasovasostomy, vasoepididymostomy, sperm recovery, and the technical decisions that shape reproductive outcomes.

03

Sexual Medicine

Clinical scholarship across erectile dysfunction, priapism, penile prosthetic surgery, Peyronie's disease, and restoration of function after cancer or chronic disease.

04

Informatics And Care Delivery

Interest in tools, telemedicine, risk communication, and digital systems that make complex clinical decisions easier to understand and act on.
